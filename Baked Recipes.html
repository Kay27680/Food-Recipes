Red Velvet Cupcakes

Bake a batch of red velvet cupcakes as a treat. With chocolate sponge and a cream cheese topping, they're perfect for elevenses served with a cuppa

Ingredients

150g plain flour

1 tbsp cocoa powder

1 tsp bicarbonate of soda

50g butter , softened

150g caster sugar

1 large egg , beaten

1 tsp vanilla paste

100ml buttermilk or kefir

50ml vegetable oil

1 tsp white wine vinegar

1 tbsp red gel food colouring

For the cream cheese icing: 100g slightly salted butter , softened

225g icing sugar

100g full fat cream cheese , stirred to loosen

Doughnut muffins

Delight friends with an afternoon tea that includes this easy carrot cake. You can bake the cake, freeze it and just drizzle over the icing on the day

Ingredients

175g light muscovado sugar

3 large eggs, lightly beaten

100g raisins

1 large orange, zested

175g self-raising flour

1 tsp bicarbonate of soda

1 tsp ground cinnamon

½ tsp grated nutmeg (freshly grated will give you the best flavour)
